Senior Expert Engineer, Software Support to Customers
I entered Safran group in 2001, where I worked in the Secure Terminals R&D unit as a software engineer. Here are the products I worked on:
- Document scanner equipped with smart card and magnetic card readers. I was in charge of the firmware of this complex USB device, and also of its MS Windows test application.
- Smart card IC, used for healthcare and identity applications. I developed together with a team a strongly secured embedded code.

I switched in 2007 to Wavecom company for a position of engineer with a focus on customer applications.
Sierra Wireless acquired Wavecom on 2008, which is consequently my current company.

I am providing the company with software-level product expertise, system integration knowledge, and support to software development.

The different wireless modules and solutions we deliver are adding these features to customer products:

GSM/GPRS/EDGE/CDMA/3G/HSxPA/LTE/5G, GNSS, WLAN/Blueooth, Ethernet/USB/PCI/SD/SDIO/MMC, audio, IP protocols, security features, over-the-air management, embedded Linux environment.
